About

VFX
COMPOSITOR
& 3D ARTIST

VFX Compositor with extensive experience across feature films, streaming series, and commercial productions. Led compositing and FX on major productions including Medusa (Netflix, 2025), Pancho Villa: El Centauro del Norte (Disney+), and Outer Range 2 (Prime Video).

Specializing in seamless CG integration, environment design, and FX simulation. From underwater yacht destruction sequences and full‑CG creature integration, to dystopian Unreal Engine city environments — I bring complex visual effects to life with photorealistic precision.

Currently expanding into large‑scale FX including Houdini‑based explosion simulations, fracturing, and fluid dynamics.
